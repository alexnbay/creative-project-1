About the Band

Radio Silenceis a group originating from the southern end of Manchester. Jonathon Doolitle, guitarist and vocalist, and his sister Hailey, bassist,found their third member in drummer Eddie Greene at Manchester University, and, inspired by bands such as Slowdive, Beach House and Pinegrove, decided to quit school and follow their dream of performing.

Jonathon Doolitle is a guitarist and vocalist. He ascribes his critically acclaimed fingerpicking style to a mix of following his idols and happy accidents. "Sometimes it just happens, you know? A slip there, a slide here--somehow it turns out." He would love to have countryman Ben Howard perform with the group someday. "I know you're out there, Ben!"

Hailey Doolitle is Radio Silence's bassist. When asked about her dreams and aspirations, she replied "Sod off." Her opinion on Radio Silence's critically acclaimed discography is that it is "bloody shite". If she could have anybody featured on the next album, she would pick Lucifer himself.
